About Lit Nation

Lit Nation is a collaborative fiction community focused on allowing authors to generate stories together. By offering planning tools and a space to write together, we hope to inspire more aspiring writers to band together to create exciting worlds. Don't worry about quality, quantity, or subject matter. We welcome stories of all sorts; short and long, original and fanfic, beginner and advanced. The categories speak for themselves. If it's fiction and it's yours, it belongs!

Our planning tools should allow you to get a good start by providing a space to develop characters, settings, and plots. They will also help any contributing authors you may decide to join forces with to write alongside you and stay true to your vision for your own story aspects. Our collaborative editor allows multiple authors to write together and form one cohesive story while chatting realtime.

Interested in writing together? Then join now!
